# job-counsultancy
A modern, responsive landing page for a premier job consultancy agency. Features career counseling insights, job placement services, resume-building highlights, and an interactive portal designed to connect top talent with industry-leading employers.
Dream Career Job Consultancy Landing Page
This repository contains the front-end code for Dream Career, a sleek and user-centric job consultancy landing page. The platform is designed to bridge the gap between ambitious job seekers and top-tier employers, offering a comprehensive overview of recruitment services, career coaching, and placement opportunities.

Key Features:
Job Board & Placements: Displays active career tracks and job openings across diverse industries (Tech, Finance, Marketing, Healthcare, etc.).

Career Counseling Hub: Highlights dedicated services for resume screening, interview preparation, and personalized career path guidance.

Employer Portal Overview: Showcases corporate recruitment solutions, detailing how companies can partner to find verified talent.

Interactive Form / CTA: Streamlined "Apply Now" or "Upload Resume" calls-to-action to maximize user conversion and candidate registration.

Fully Responsive Design: Optimally styled for flawless navigation across desktops, tablets, and mobile devices.

 Target Audience:
Job seekers looking to elevate their career paths, fresh graduates seeking guidance, and HR professionals/employers searching for recruitment partners.
